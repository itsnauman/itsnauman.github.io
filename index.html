Nauman Ahmad

I'm a Software Engineer at Meta, where I work on coding agents and AI developer tools that help engineers build software at the speed of thought.

I grew up in Pakistan and later studied Computer Science at New York University. Since then, I've lived in New York, San Francisco, and Vancouver, places that have each shaped how I think about technology, people, and the world around us.

I'm interested in AI, science, history, politics, geopolitics, and chess. I'm drawn to the questions that connect these areas, including how people reason, how systems change over time, how incentives shape behavior, and how technology affects the way we think and live.
