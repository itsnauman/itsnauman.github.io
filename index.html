Nauman Ahmad

I'm a Software Engineer at Meta, where I work on coding agents and AI developer tools that help engineers build software at the speed of thought.

I grew up in Pakistan and later studied Computer Science at New York University. Since then, I’ve lived in New York, San Francisco, and Vancouver. Each place has expanded the way I see technology, culture, and the human story.

I’m interested in AI, science, history, political philosophy, and chess. I’m drawn to the deeper questions that connect them: how people reason, how systems evolve, how incentives shape behavior, and how technology changes the way we think, create, and live.

I’m fascinated by ideas that connect different fields, from the logic of machines to the patterns of history, and from human nature to the deeper structure of reality. I’m drawn to first principles, broad perspective, and the kind of understanding that makes the world feel a little more intelligible.
